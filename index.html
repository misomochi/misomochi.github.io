Projects

Motion Estimation & Motion Compensation (MEMC) Circuit

CVSD, Fall 2019

An implementation of block-based MEMC algorithm by Verilog and digital IC design workflow

  • The circuit is operable on 10 sequential 8-bit 48×64 pixels gray level images
  • Block matching method by mean absolute error is carried out to determine the best matching block (8×8 pixels) of the current frame corresponding with that of the previous frame within the search range of 7 pixels. The result is denoted as the motion vector and calculated into peak signa-to-noise ratio (PSNR)
  • Performed the digital IC design workflow, i.e., RTL design, synthesis, place & route, post-layout verification, etc.
